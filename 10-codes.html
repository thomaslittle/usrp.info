Standard Call Outs

Enroute

Announcing you are heading to a call.

(CallSign) (+1) 10-76 [Code 1/2/3]

On Scene

Announcing you have arrived at the call location.

(CallSign) 10-23

Done with Call

Announcing you have completed the call and are available again.

(CallSign) 10-24, returning 10-8
